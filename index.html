Why This Plan

Built after two injury setbacks interrupted the 2026 Ballarat campaign — a Feb 2026 thoracic injury from 4 high-quality sessions in 3 weeks on 100km weeks, and an April Cairns rep session that spiked intensity onto unconditioned tendons after a low-volume period.
The plan's core architecture exists specifically to prevent repeating those two failure modes:
One quality session per week (Wednesday only)
Never back-to-back hard days
Max 8% weekly volume increase
Max 2km long run increase per step
Single variable in quality reps per week (count OR distance, never both)
Recovery weeks every 4th week, non-negotiable (W7, W16, W19)
